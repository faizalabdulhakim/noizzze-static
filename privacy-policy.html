Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Noizzze ("we," "our," or "the app") handles your information. Noizzze is a white noise and ambient sound app that lets you mix and play relaxing sounds.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Google Sign-In

When you choose to sign in with your Google account, we collect the following information from your Google profile:

  • Display name — as shown on your Google account
  • Email address — your Gmail or Google-associated email
  • Profile photo URL — a link to your public Google profile picture
  • Firebase UID — a unique, anonymized identifier assigned by Firebase Authentication

1.2. Guest Mode

If you choose "Continue as Guest," we do not collect or store any personal information. You can use all free features of the app without providing any data.

1.3. Premium Purchase

When you purchase the premium upgrade, all payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect, store, or transmit your credit card number, billing address, or any other payment details. We store only a flag indicating whether your account has premium access.

2. App Permissions

Noizzze requests the following Android permissions and uses them only as described:

  • INTERNET — required for Google Sign-In, loading sound data, and communicating with our backend server.
  • WAKE_LOCK — keeps the device awake while audio is playing so your sounds continue when the screen turns off.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_AUDIO — allows the app to read audio files from your device if you choose to play local files.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — enables background audio playback so sounds keep playing while you use other apps.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — shows a media playback notification with play/pause controls during active playback.

Noizzze does not request or access: location, contacts, camera, microphone, phone state, SMS, or device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through Google Sign-In solely to:

  • Create and identify your account on our service
  • Display your name and profile picture within the app
  • Associate your premium purchase with your account so you can restore it on other devices

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making.

4. Data Storage and Transmission

Your account information (name, email, profile photo URL, user ID, and premium status) is stored:

  • On your device — in the app's local storage (SharedPreferences)
  • On our backend server — at api.noizzze.app, to support account restoration across devices

Audio sounds and mixes are loaded from our server but contain no personal information.

5. Android Auto Backup

Android devices with Google Backup enabled may back up app data (including account information stored in SharedPreferences) to Google Drive. This is controlled by your device's system settings, not by Noizzze. You can disable automatic backup in your Android Settings under System > Backup.

6. Third-Party Services

Noizzze integrates the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) — used for Google Sign-In. Firebase processes your Google account credentials to authenticate you. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing (Google LLC) — processes in-app purchases. All payment data is handled by Google; we never see or store your payment details. Google Payments Privacy Notice

7. Analytics and Tracking

Noizzze does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage, collect behavioral data, or use cookies. There is no Firebase Analytics, no Google AdMob, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

8. Children's Privacy

Noizzze is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Data Security

All communication between the app and our backend server is encrypted using HTTPS. We store the minimum amount of data necessary to provide the service and do not share your personal information with any third parties except as necessary to provide the services described above (Firebase for authentication, Google Play for billing).

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — view your account information in the Settings screen of the app
  • Delete your data — log out and uninstall the app to remove local data, or contact us to request deletion of your server-side data
  • Use the app without an account — choose "Continue as Guest" to use Noizzze without providing any personal information

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
